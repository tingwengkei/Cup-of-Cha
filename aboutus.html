ABOUT US

UP-AND-COMING CAFE

We offer a unique cafe environment unlike any other in Petaling Jaya. We are not only a place to drop in and get your morning cup of coffee (but please feel free to do so), we are a place where you can sit down and enjoy that tailor-made cup of coffee or tea. If you need to work, we have a seating area created specifically for you. If you need to rest, we have a soft-seating area in front of a wooden fire place that is perfect for your drowsy mind and body.

We offer a delicious variety of coffee made by our professionally trained baristas. We have everything from classic coffee to our house made specialty beverages. You can complete your coffee with one of our delicious sweet treats made by our very own baker. We look forward to serving you at Cup Of Cha!
