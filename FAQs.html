FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know about Cup Of Cha

Where is Cup Of Cha from?

Originated from Malaysia.


Where are your outlets?

Please refer to our outlet listing here


Where do you source the ingredients from?

We source our ingredients from a mixture of international and local products.


What are your operating hours?

We are open every Monday to Friday from 7 AM until 10 pm. For weekends as well as public holidays, we open from 7 AM to 11 PM.


Can I get a free donut if I purchase two drinks?

As long as both are coffee.


Do you serve gluten-free food?

Sorry, we do not serve any gluten-free food at the moment. But keep your fingers crossed because it could happen in the future


Do you accept credit cards?

We accept Visa Cards, Master Cards and Debit Cards.


Do customers get free Wi-Fi?

We offer Wi-Fi to all of our customers free of charge. Each customer is provided with our wireless Internet password on his or her receipt.
